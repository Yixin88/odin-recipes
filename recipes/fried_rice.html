Perfect way to make Egg Fried Rice!

Description

This will be the easiest way to make egg fried rice at home! With some basic ingredients you can find in your local supermarket. It would only take you 15 mins start to finish!

Here are some of the ingredients you will need:

  • 2 eggs
  • Left-over rice
  • Soy Sauce
  • Spring Onion
  • Sweetcore
  • Green Peas

Steps

  1. Make scrambled eggs in the pan

  2. Put in Sweetcorn, Green Peas, cook for 5 mins

  3. Put in rice and seperate it as much as possible

  4. Add soy sauce and stir until its all brown for 2 mins

  5. That's done!
